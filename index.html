About

Premed Checklist helps pre-med students quickly determine whether the courses they have taken meet prerequisite requirements for different medical schools. Simply enter your completed classes and we compare them against school requirements to help you plan your academic path.

Features

  • Check prerequisites for multiple medical schools
  • Compare your coursework against admissions requirements
  • Identify missing prerequisites early
  • Plan future semesters strategically
